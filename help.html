Fáilte

How to use this website:

Translations:

If you are using a laptop/PC, hover your mouse cursor over an Irish sentence to view the English translation.

If you are using a mobile device, tap an Irish sentence to view the English translation. Tap another spot on the page to dismiss the translation.

Here is an example: sampla

When you create an account:

Bookmarks:

Each article has a bookmark symbol. Click an empty bookmark to save the article and page to easily resume reading.

Click a full bookmark to remove a bookmark. You can also remove a bookmark in "My Account".

Word Bank:

Each article has an accompanying Foclóir section.

Each word in the list can be added to your Word Bank to build up your vocabulary.
